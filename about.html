About Me

I am 22 and doing what I love.

I began my four years at St. Edward's University in the fall of 2013. I graduated in 2017 with a major in Catholic Studies and a minor in Music. During my time at the university, I participated in the university choir, where I had many opportunities to sing in Austin and around the globe. I also worked as a teaching assistant for the choir, where I gained valuable leadership experience. In my final year of college, I began to actively pursue my desire to become a personal trainer. I began studying to be certified and I was hired as a personal trainer for my university health program. As a trainer, I worked with multiple clients throughout the school semesters and helped them reach their fitness goals. I currently work at HEAT Bootcamp in addition to training, and I am looking forward to continuing to grow my knowledge and my love for fitness.

I like singing and taking photos.

While I am focused on my career, I have other hobbies that I enjoy doing in my spare time. Choir and singing has been a part of my life since I was a child - I always dreamed about become a famous musician (admittedly, I still do). I currently sing with my good friends at St. Theresa Catholic Church. I also love taking photos. Head on over to my instagram page if you are interested.

Austin is my home.
